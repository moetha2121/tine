More About Me

Born in Myanmar but raised in Singapore, I have been immersed in the Singaporean education system from a young age. Growing up with two distinct cultures has provided me with a unique perspective and a deep appreciation for diverse viewpoints. My journey from Myanmar to Singapore has been significantly enriched by the friendships I have formed along the way. My friends have always been accepting of my background and have consistently included me in their activities. Their inclusivity has made me feel welcomed and valued, enabling me to adapt and thrive in a new environment.

Why I chose IT/Cybersecurity

In secondary school, I did a lot of studying with textbooks. When I got my results and looked at different schools, I found the IT field really interesting and exciting. Learning about hacking and how computers work caught my attention. It's amazing to understand what goes on inside a computer, and this has made me passionate about pursuing a career in IT.
